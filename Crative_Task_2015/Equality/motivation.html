Letter of Motivation

Ever since I decided to work in the educational sector I enjoyed to be with people whether I’ve been with children, juveniles, adults or elderly. I collected pedagogical experience along the way, that I can utilise in my projects.

My passion for design, software development and programming grew stronger. So much so that I come to the point that I’d love to work in mobile creative world.

I set my focus on the newest programming and mockup languages to build up-to-date styled apps and websites. Constantly learning and expanding my knowledge is great fun for me. This striving for aesthetics, innovation and constant progression is shaping my life.

Programming chanced how I think and it combines problem solving with my experience. I find this dynamic environment in mobile software engineering immensely appealing. To start with a blank sheet, designing an application from scratch evokes creativity in me.

To study at Hyper Island would greatly accelerate my journey to become an app developer. I see this vocational school as a network of students, alumni and teachers as well as a direct connection to the corporate world.

Contacts that would result in once in a lifetime chances is what I’ll seek there. Agility in manufacturing ideas and capability in picking the successful ones is knowledge I wish to gain. I believe that I’d find that at Hyper Island.
