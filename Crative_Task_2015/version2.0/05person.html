Disorders and Equality

Disorders are gradual phenomena, not black-and-white criteria by means of which to put people into boxes, especially when it comes to access of technological means to improve our quality of life. Therefore it is not good enough to provide some people with that access while leaving others in the cold. Technology is a powerful means to level unequal playing fields, but in order to do so, it must be available to all, equally.
