What Are My Values?

I live by them, believe in them, but haven't brought them into my consciousness.

With my first google search did I find some nice sticker images about values education.

My built in apple dictionay provides me with this description of the meaning of value:

value |ˈvaljuː|

noun

1 [ mass noun ] the regard that something is held to deserve; the importance, worth, or usefulness of something: your support is of great value.

• the material or monetary worth of something: prints seldom rise in value | [ count noun ] : equipment is included up to a total value of £500.

• the worth of something compared to the price paid or asked for it: at £12.50 the book is good value | [ count noun ] N. Amer. : the wine represents a good value for $17.95.

2 (values) principles or standards of behaviour; one's judgement of what is important in life: they internalize their parents' rules and values.

Personal Values

So personal values are principles or standards of behavior I want to life by. A moral code that I find important to have in life. A way of doing that I might been growing up with, but more and more a conscious choice of actions that I want to stand for. Values I want to have and values that make me having good attitude, so that I can be proud of my self.

Those personal values I carry with and for myself might be what I want it to be. But what happens when my choices of action do affect others? What happens when values extend indivituals and are taken on in different groups with different interests?

Refugee Crtsis

There have never been so many refugees and displaced persons according to the United Nations. And in Europe have never been so many refugees seeking for asylum since befor the Second World War.

I grew up in Europe and life here with all those benefits and laws that support me but not those who seek for asylum. What can I do to for those people that search for a new home? I donated to the Red Cross. I have been happy that fellow students started the #hihumanity campaign. At least can I have good values to guide me.

But can I set actions that have an impact to help us with dealing with this change that the humans on european ground are confronted with? How can I stand up and show my good values that I reflect upon?

I feel a strong urge to voice my opinion on essential values I see as important in this times of change. Whom do I want to talk to? Who shall read my thoughts and opinions?

Still, what are my values?
