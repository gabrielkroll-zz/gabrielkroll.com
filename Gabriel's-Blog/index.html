Personal Values

Personal values are principles or standards of behaviour I want to live by. It’s a moral code that I find important to have in life. I grew up with certain rules around me and hopefully those where based on good values like being creative, playful, honest, kind, respectful, caring and likewise.

Every now and then where my values adjusted by new beliefs and experiences. My surroundings shaped and even inspired me to become what I am today. My friends supported or challenged my patterns. Adaptions are happening partly unconsciously but also through reflection on life. More and more does it become a conscious choice for me what values to uphold and what beliefs I want to discard. The more I make an effort to reflect on my actions the better I know about my values.

There are long lists of attributes and adjectives that show many nuances I can take on. If somebody asks my what are my values, than I’d most likely be struggling to answer directly. I’d rather like to think and reflect for some time to be able to answer carefully.

What have I done in my life? What do I belief in? Do I act 1on my values? Am I persistent with my actions? What are my core values?

There is a lot of room for deep reflections. And there are hundreds of possible attributes that might resonate with me.
